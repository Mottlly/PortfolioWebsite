Seekit: The Pantry Manager

Get Coding - Halifax, NS

A pantry management system, with the goal of reducing food waste on behalf of both of your pocketbook and the environment.

Features Include:

  • Sortable locally stored pantry storage database
  • Dashboard that highlights food items close to expiry, and expired.
  • Recipe search function powered by Edamam, to search for recipes by Keyword

Planned Features Include:

  • Recipe search function to include dietary preferences, and focus on close-to-expire foods
  • Push notifications on mobile about expiring food items
  • Future purchasing recommendations based on likelihood of specific foods to go bad

4X Game (WIP)

Personal Project - Halifax, NS

A sci-fi survival 4X strategy game set on a hostile alien world, where players must explore, expand, exploit, and exterminate — all while managing scarce resources and surviving the elements.

Planned Features Include:

  • Hex-based map system with procedurally generated terrain and resource nodes
  • Survival mechanics including food, energy, and weather hazards
  • Faction-based gameplay with unique traits and technologies
  • Turn-based exploration and combat, with fog of war and unit customization
  • Tech tree progression that adapts to your environment and faction playstyle
  • Dynamic events like alien fauna invasions, solar flares, and supply shortages
  • In-depth colony and outpost building with morale and population management

In Obscurum

Personal Project - Halifax, NS

A sci-fi survival role-playing game inspired by classic choose-your-own-adventure stories. As the captain of a deep-space expedition, you must lead humanity’s last hope into the unknown — and, with luck, bring them home.

Survive the terrors of deep space by managing your ship, your crew, and your choices:

  • Create a custom character with unique backgrounds, skills, and AI-generated portraits
  • Make difficult command decisions to keep your expedition supplied, sane, and alive
  • Experience shifting visuals and multiple endings shaped by your choices and outcomes
