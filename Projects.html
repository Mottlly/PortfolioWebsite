Seekit: The Pantry Manager

Get Coding - Halifax, NS

A pantry management system, with the goal of reducing food waste on behalf of both of your pocketbook and the environment.

Features Include:

  • Sortable locally stored pantry storage database
  • Dashboard that highlights food items close to expiry, and expired.
  • Recipe search function powered by Edamam, to search for recipes by Keyword

Planned Features Include:

  • Recipe search function to include dietary preferences, and focus on close-to-expire foods
  • Push notifications on mobile about expiring food items
  • Future purchasing recommendations based on likelihood of specific foods to go bad

In Obscurum

Personal Project - Halifax, NS

A Sci-Fi survival role playing game in the style of ye olde choose-your-own-adventure stories. Lead an expedition into the darkness to discover humanity's salvation and, hopefully, return alive.

You will need to manage your ship and crew skillfully to survive the terrors of deep space:

  • Choose your character's background, skills, and generate their appearance utilizing AI
  • As captain, make the right decisions to keep your expedition well-supplied, motivated, and alive
  • Experience a dynamically changing art and ending based on your choices, for better or for worse
